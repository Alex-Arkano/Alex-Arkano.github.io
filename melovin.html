Melovin made a comingout at Atlas Weekend. How did Ukrainians react to this?

He stated that for him it does not matter "color, faith or gender" of the person you love, because you need to love "a person, body, soul."

On stage, Melovin passionately kissed a girl and then a guy and went out to the audience with an LGBT flag.

Later on his social networks, the singer expressed indignation that his act, which he called the "official comingout", was cut from the live broadcast of M1, which broadcasts Atlas Weekend concerts.

StarLightMedia media holding, which includes the M1 channel, responded to these accusations. They said that the channel was not informed about the planned moment and the broadcast was switched to other scenes after the performance "for the sake of greater dynamism and brightness of the frame."

At the same time, the media holding welcomed the Melovin comingout.

"This is a manifestation of the right to freedom of expression and a sign of a democratic society. We are pleased to welcome the action of Melovin, who made one of the first such statements among public figures in Ukraine.
