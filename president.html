The Haitian president was assassinated in his own home. The first lady was wounded

Haitian President Joven Moise, 53, was assassinated at his private residence on Wednesday morning.

This was announced by Acting the Prime Minister of Haiti Claude Joseph.

The murder happened around 1:00 am local time. According to the previous version, it was carried out by a group of attackers, said the Prime Minister.

The identities of the attackers have not yet been established.

The first lady was also wounded by a firearm during the attack.

Mr Joseph said that all measures had been taken to ensure stability in the country. Juvenile Mois had been in power since February 2017. Haiti is one of the poorest countries in the Western Hemisphere. Its population is about 10.6 million people. In February last year, protests began in the country, whose participants demanded the resignation of Moise.
