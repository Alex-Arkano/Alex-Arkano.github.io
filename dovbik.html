Not a bra, but a device. What Dovbyk wore under his T-shirt

After the decisive goal for the Swedes, Artem Dovbyk happily dropped his T-shirt, and everyone saw a black top on it.

In fact, this is not a "bra", as they began to joke on social networks, but a special sports device.

Apex Athlete Series is a GPS tracker from STATSports.

The device allows you to measure how many kilometers a particular player ran for the entire game, what was the maximum speed, sprint distance and when he started to get tired.

Based on this data, they also make a "thermal map" of the football player on the field, which shows where he was most often during the match.

Such devices help coaching staffs to analyze the actions of each player.

The kit includes the same black top, GPS and a program that transmits and processes data in real time
